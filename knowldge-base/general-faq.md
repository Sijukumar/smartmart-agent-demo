# SmartMart General FAQ

Last Updated: June 2026
Version: 2.0

## Account & Profile

**Q: How do I create a SmartMart account?**
A: Visit smartmart-demo.com or download the SmartMart app. Click "Sign Up" and register with your email or phone number. Verification takes less than 1 minute.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: No. Each person is limited to one SmartMart account. Multiple accounts may be suspended without notice.

**Q: How do I update my personal information?**
A: Go to Settings → Personal Information. You can update your name, email, phone number, and delivery addresses. Email changes require verification.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Your account will be deactivated for 30 days (during which you can reactivate it), then permanently deleted. Active orders must be completed or cancelled first.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email or phone number. You'll receive a reset link/code valid for 15 minutes.

## Orders

**Q: How do I place an order?**
A: Browse products → add to cart → proceed to checkout → enter delivery address → select shipping method → choose payment → confirm order.

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
A: You can modify your order within 30 minutes of placement (before it enters "Processing" status). Changes include adding/removing items, updating address, and changing shipping method. After processing begins, you'll need to cancel and reorder.

**Q: How do I cancel my order?**
A: Go to "My Orders" → select the order → click "Cancel Order." If the order hasn't shipped, it will be cancelled immediately with a full refund. If already shipped, you'll need to follow the return process.

**Q: What is order confirmation?**
A: After placing an order, you'll receive an order confirmation email/SMS with your order number, item summary, and estimated delivery date. If you don't receive confirmation within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

**Q: How long does it take to receive my order?**
A: Depends on shipping method: Same-day (order before 12pm), Next-day, Express (1-2 days), Standard (3-5 days). International orders take 5-28 days depending on destination and method.

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes! Go to "My Orders" and click "Track Package" for real-time updates. You'll also receive email/SMS notifications at each stage.

## Products & Availability

**Q: Are all products on SmartMart genuine?**
A: Yes. SmartMart sources directly from authorized distributors and brand partners. We guarantee 100% authentic products. Counterfeit items are prohibited on our platform.

**Q: What does "Low Stock" mean?**
A: "Low Stock" means fewer than 10 units remain. We recommend purchasing quickly as the item may sell out soon.

**Q: When will an out-of-stock item be available again?**
A: Check the product page for estimated restock dates. You can click "Notify Me" to receive an email/push notification when the item is back in stock.

**Q: Do product images accurately represent the item?**
A: We strive for accurate product photography. Slight color variations may occur due to screen settings. Product dimensions and specifications are listed on each product page.

**Q: Can I reserve an item?**
A: We don't offer item reservations. Items are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Add to cart and checkout to secure your item.

## SmartMart Membership

**Q: How do I check my membership tier?**
A: Go to your profile → "My Membership." Your current tier, annual spend, and benefits are displayed.

**Q: When does my tier status reset?**
A: Tier status is evaluated annually on January 1st based on the previous 12 months' spend. If your spend qualifies for a higher tier mid-year, you're upgraded immediately.

**Q: Can I earn tier status faster?**
A: Yes. Double-points promotions run periodically (usually during major sales events). SmartMart Wallet purchases earn 1% bonus toward tier qualification.

## Customer Support

**Q: What are your support hours?**
A: Web Chat: 24/7 (AI-powered Smart Assistant). Phone: Mon–Sat, 9am–9pm SGT. Email: Response within 24 hours.

**Q: How do I contact a human agent?**
A: Start with the Web Chat. If the Smart Assistant cannot resolve your issue, type "speak to a human" or "transfer to agent" and you'll be connected to a live representative during support hours.

**Q: What is the Smart Assistant?**
A: The Smart Assistant is our AI-powered chatbot available 24/7 on the SmartMart app and website. It can help with order tracking, product questions, returns, shipping inquiries, and general FAQs. For complex issues, it seamlessly transfers you to a human agent.

**Q: How do I provide feedback about my experience?**
A: After each support interaction, you'll receive a satisfaction survey. You can also leave product reviews, rate deliveries, and provide general feedback under Settings → Feedback.

## Privacy & Security

**Q: How does SmartMart protect my data?**
A: We use industry-standard encryption (256-bit SSL/TLS), comply with Singapore PDPA, and never share personal data with third parties without consent. Read our full Privacy Policy at smartmart-demo.com/privacy.

**Q: Does SmartMart store my credit card details?**
A: Only if you opt in. Card details are tokenized and stored by our PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. SmartMart never has access to your full card number.

**Q: How do I report suspicious activity on my account?**
A: Contact support immediately at +65-6789-1234 or email security@smartmart-demo.com. Change your password and enable two-factor authentication.

**Q: Does SmartMart use my data for advertising?**
A: With your consent only. You can manage advertising preferences under Settings → Privacy → Ad Preferences. Opting out does not affect your shopping experience.

## Sustainability & Ethics

**Q: Does SmartMart have sustainable packaging options?**
A: Yes. We use recycled cardboard, biodegradable fill, and minimal plastic. Select "Eco-Friendly Packaging" at checkout for consolidated shipping (may take 1-2 additional days).

**Q: Does SmartMart verify ethical sourcing?**
A: We require all marketplace sellers to comply with our Ethical Sourcing Policy. Direct suppliers undergo annual audits covering labor practices, environmental standards, and safety compliance.

## Technical Issues

**Q: The SmartMart app is crashing. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps: (1) Force close and reopen, (2) Clear app cache, (3) Update to the latest version, (4) Restart your phone, (5) Uninstall and reinstall. If the issue persists, contact support with your device model and OS version.

**Q: I can't add items to my cart.**
A: This may happen if the item is out of stock, you've reached the maximum quantity per customer, or there's a temporary system issue. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

**Q: Why can't I log in?**
A: Common causes: incorrect password (use "Forgot Password" to reset), account locked (too many failed attempts — wait 30 minutes or contact support), account suspended (contact support for details).
