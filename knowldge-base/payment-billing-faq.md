## SmartMart Wallet

### What is SmartMart Wallet?
SmartMart Wallet is a digital wallet within your SmartMart account. You can top up your wallet balance and use it for faster checkout. Refunds to SmartMart Wallet are processed instantly.

### Top-Up Methods
- Credit/Debit Card (min $10, max $5,000)
- Bank Transfer (min $50, no maximum)
- PayPal (min $10, max $2,000)

### Wallet Benefits
- Instant refunds (no waiting for bank processing)
- Exclusive wallet-only deals and cashback
- 1% cashback on all wallet purchases (capped at $20/month)
- No expiry on wallet balance

### Withdrawal
- Withdraw to linked bank account (min $50)
- Processing time: 3–5 business days
- No withdrawal fee

## Pricing & Currency

### Currency
- All prices displayed in USD
- Currency conversion handled by your bank/payment provider

### Price Match
SmartMart does not offer a price-match guarantee. However, if an item you purchased goes on sale within 7 days of purchase, you can request a price adjustment through Customer Support.

### Tax & GST
- Singapore: 9% GST included in displayed price
- Malaysia: Sales Tax included in displayed price

## Billing Issues

### Common Billing Questions

**Q: I was charged twice for my order.**
A: Duplicate charges are usually temporary authorization holds. They typically disappear within 3–5 business days. If the charge persists beyond 5 days, contact us with your order number and bank statement screenshot.

**Q: My payment was declined. Why?**
A: Common reasons include insufficient funds, card expired, bank security block (international transactions), incorrect card details, or daily spending limit reached. Try a different payment method or contact your bank.

**Q: I see a charge I don't recognize from SmartMart.**
A: Check your order history for matching amounts. The charge may appear as "SMARTMART SG" or "SM*SMARTMART" on your statement. If no match found, contact support immediately.

**Q: Can I change my payment method after placing an order?**
A: Once an order is placed and payment is processed, you cannot change the payment method. You would need to cancel the order and place a new one.

**Q: When am I charged for pre-orders?**
A: Pre-orders are charged at the time of order placement, not at shipment. If the price drops before release, you'll receive the lower price.
