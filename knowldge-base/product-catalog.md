## Product Availability & Restocking

- **In Stock:** Ships within 1–2 business days.
- **Low Stock:** Limited quantity; may sell out quickly. Orders fulfilled on first-come basis.
- **Out of Stock:** Expected restock date shown on product page. Backorders accepted with estimated delivery.
- **Pre-Order:** Available for select new releases. Charged at time of order; ships on release date.

Restocking typically occurs every 2 weeks. High-demand items (new phone releases, seasonal fashion) may take 4–6 weeks to restock.
