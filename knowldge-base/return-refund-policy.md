# SmartMart Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026
Version: 2.1

---

## Overview

At SmartMart, we want you to be satisfied with every purchase. If you're not happy with your order, our return policy makes it easy to send items back for a refund or exchange.

---

## Return Window

| Item Category | Return Window | Condition Required |
|--------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Electronics | 14 days | Original packaging, all accessories |
| Fashion & Apparel | 30 days | Unworn, tags attached |
| Home & Kitchen | 30 days | Unused, original packaging |
| Sports & Outdoors | 30 days | Unused, original packaging |
| Books | 14 days | Unopened (physical) / 14-day refund (digital) |
| Bundles | 14 days | All items returned together |

---

## Eligibility Criteria

### Items Eligible for Return

- Products received damaged or defective
- Wrong item delivered
- Items that don't match the product description
- Fashion items that don't fit (unworn, tags attached)
- Electronics with manufacturing defects

### Items NOT Eligible for Return

- Gift cards and digital vouchers
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Undergarments and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Items returned after the return window has expired
- Items showing signs of use, wear, or damage by customer
- Opened software, video games, and digital downloads
- Perishable goods (if applicable)

## How to Initiate a Return

### Step 1: Request a Return

1. Log into your SmartMart account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order containing the item you want to return
4. Click "Return or Replace Item"
5. Choose the reason for return
6. Select your preferred resolution (refund or exchange)

### Step 2: Pack the Item

- Use the original packaging whenever possible
- Include all accessories, manuals, and free gifts that came with the item
- Attach the return shipping label (printed or digital — see below)
- Remove or cover any old shipping labels

### Step 3: Ship the Item

Choose one of these options:

| Method | Cost | Processing Time |
|--------|------|----------------|
| Drop-off at SmartMart Pick-Up Point | Free | 2–3 business days |
| Scheduled Home Pick-Up | $4.99 | 1–2 business days |
| Self-Ship (use any courier) | Customer pays | 3–5 business days |

## Refund Process

### Timeline

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Return received at warehouse | 1–2 business days |
| Quality inspection | 1–2 business days |
| Refund processed | 1–3 business days |
| Refund appears in your account | 3–10 business days (depends on bank) |

**Total expected time: 5–15 business days** from the date we receive your return.

### Refund Method

| Original Payment Method | Refund Method |
|------------------------|---------------|
| Credit/Debit Card | Same card used for purchase |
| SmartMart Wallet | SmartMart Wallet (instant) |
| Bank Transfer | Same bank account |
| Cash on Delivery | SmartMart Wallet or bank transfer |
| Gift Card | New gift card of equal value |

### Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued if:
- The item is returned in a condition different from original
- Missing accessories or packaging
- Item shows signs of use beyond normal inspection

The partial refund amount will be communicated to you before processing. You may choose to have the item returned to you instead.

---

## Exchanges

### How Exchanges Work

1. Request an exchange through "My Orders" (same process as return)
2. Specify the desired replacement (size, color, or different product)
3. Ship the original item back
4. Once received and inspected, we ship the replacement

**If the replacement item costs more:** You'll be charged the difference.
**If the replacement item costs less:** The difference is refunded to your original payment method.

### Exchange Limitations

- Only one exchange per item
- Replacement must be the same product (different size/color is okay)
- If the desired product is out of stock, a refund will be issued instead
- Exchanges are not available for clearance or final-sale items

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

1. **Report within 48 hours** of delivery via the app or website
2. Take photos of the damage and packaging
3. Upload photos with your return request
4. SmartMart covers return shipping for verified damage claims
5. Choose between replacement or full refund

### Manufacturer Defects (Electronics)

- Covered under the 1-year manufacturer warranty
- Contact SmartMart Support within 14 days for immediate replacement
- After 14 days, warranty claims go through the manufacturer
- SmartMart can assist with warranty claim filing

## Cancellation Policy

| Order Status | Cancellation | Refund |
|-------------|--------------|--------|
| Not yet shipped | Full cancellation | Full refund within 24 hours |
| Shipped but not delivered | Cancel + intercept shipment | Full refund (5–7 days) |
| Delivered | Return process applies | Per return policy above |

**Pre-orders** can be cancelled at any time before shipping for a full refund.

## Special Circumstances

### Holiday Returns

During November–December, the return window is extended:
- Items purchased between Nov 1 and Dec 24 can be returned until **January 31**
- Standard return conditions still apply

### International Orders

- Return shipping is at the customer's expense for international orders
- Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable
- SmartMart wallet refunds are available for international customers (instant credit)

### Marketplace Sellers

Products sold by third-party sellers on SmartMart Marketplace may have different return policies. Check the seller's return policy on the product page before purchasing.
