## Order Processing Time

| Order Type | Processing Time |
|-----------|----------------|
| In-stock items | 1–2 business days |
| Low-stock items | 1–2 business days |
| Pre-orders | Ships on release date |
| Backorders | 2–4 weeks (estimated date provided) |

Processing time is added to the delivery timeline. For example, an in-stock item with Express Delivery = 1–2 days processing + 1–2 days delivery = 2–4 business days total.

## Order Tracking

### How to Track Your Order

1. **SmartMart App/Website:** Go to "My Orders" → click the order → "Track Package"
2. **Email Notification:** Tracking link sent when your order ships
3. **SMS Notification:** If enabled, tracking updates sent via SMS
4. **Web Chat:** Ask the Smart Assistant for real-time tracking updates

### Tracking Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Order Placed | Your order has been received |
| Processing | Being prepared for shipment |
| Shipped | Handed to courier, tracking active |
| In Transit | On the way to your location |
| Out for Delivery | With local delivery partner today |
| Delivered | Successfully delivered |
| Failed Delivery | Delivery attempt unsuccessful |
| Returned to Sender | Package being returned (unclaimed) |

## Delivery Instructions

### Special Instructions

You can add delivery instructions during checkout:
- "Leave at front door"
- "Ring doorbell"
- "Deliver to neighbor"
- "Leave with building concierge"

**Note:** SmartMart is not responsible for packages left per delivery instructions that go missing.

### Signature Required

The following orders require a signature upon delivery:
- Orders over $500
- Electronics (laptops, smartphones, tablets)
- Items marked "Signature Required" by the seller

If no one is available, the courier will:
1. Leave a notification card
2. Attempt redelivery next business day
3. After 3 attempts, return to SmartMart warehouse

## Failed Delivery & Redelivery

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Not home during delivery | Courier leaves notification; free redelivery within 3 days |
| Wrong address provided | Contact support to update before shipping; after shipping, $9.99 redirection fee |
| Package refused at door | Returned to warehouse; refund minus shipping cost |
| 3 failed delivery attempts | Package returned; full refund minus shipping |

## Shipping Restrictions

### Items That Cannot Be Shipped Internationally
- Aerosols and pressurized containers
- Lithium batteries (loose/uninstalled)
- Perishable food items
- Oversized furniture (international only)
- Items restricted by destination country's customs regulations

### Prohibited Delivery Locations
- PO Boxes (for oversized items)
- Military bases (APO/FPO) — currently not supported
- Remote/unreachable areas (checked at checkout)

## Customs, Duties & Taxes

### Who Pays?

| Charge | Responsibility |
|--------|---------------|
| Import duties | Customer |
| Customs taxes | Customer |
| Handling fees | Customer |
| VAT/GST at destination | Customer |

SmartMart displays estimated customs fees at checkout for supported destinations. Actual fees may vary based on local customs assessment.

### Customs Delays

- International packages may be held by customs for inspection
- This can add 3–10 business days to delivery time
- SmartMart has no control over customs processing times
- Contact local customs office for status updates

## Lost or Missing Packages

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received your package:

1. Check with household members, neighbors, or building concierge
2. Wait 24 hours (some carriers scan early)
3. Contact SmartMart Support with your order number
4. SmartMart will investigate with the carrier (takes 5–10 business days)
5. If confirmed lost: full refund or replacement shipped

If tracking hasn't updated in 7+ days:

1. Contact SmartMart Support
2. Carrier investigation initiated
3. If package confirmed lost: full refund or replacement
